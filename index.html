Enslaved African Muslims in the Americas

A class project for collecting and recalling the lives of enslaved Muslims in the Americas who are generally not listed or talked about in American Literature and History. This project demonstrates that Muslims, along with their culture and language, have been part of American heritage.

Primary Sources

Explore authentic documents and materials written by or about key figures like Omar Ibn Said, Nicholas Said, Job (Ayyub) the son of Solomon, and more.

Secondary Sources

Dive deeper into the history of African Muslims in the Americas through articles, videos, and research papers.
